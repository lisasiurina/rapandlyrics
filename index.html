Project Stages

Stage 01

Data Acquisition & Scraping

The initial stage involved gathering raw historical data. I developed two independent crawlers to build a robust foundation:

1. Billboard Scraper: Automated extraction of song lists and metadata from Billboard Rap Charts across three decades.
2. Wiki Scraper: Generated a "Truth Table" master list of all automotive manufacturers and models to enable precise entity recognition in lyrics.

Requests BeautifulSoup4 JSON
Stage 02

NLP & Data Cleaning

Rap lyrics are highly colloquial and noisy. The main challenge was entity disambiguation. I utilized RegEx patterns for token matching and mapping slang terms (e.g., 'Benz', 'Lac', 'Beamer') to their parent corporate brands. I implemented custom filters to handle homonyms (e.g., distinguishing 'Prince' the artist from 'Prince' the vehicle model).

Regular Expressions NLTK Tokenization
Stage 03

Exploratory Data Analysis

Using Pandas, data was aggregated by year and artist demographics. I calculated the "lyrical market share" for brands per decade. The analysis identified strong correlations between the genre's commercial evolution and the surge in mentions of European luxury brands.

Pandas NumPy Matplotlib
Stage 04

Visualization & Conclusions

The final stage translated data into actionable insights. The findings highlighted a significant shift from domestic icons (Cadillac/Chevy) to German and Italian luxury brands starting in the early 2000s, paralleling the genre's globalization.
